FAQs

most commonly asked questions

What are your hours?

We are open from 8 AM to midnight every day. Our cozy atmosphere is perfect for late-night coffee lovers. Enjoy our delicious menu until closing time!p>

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes! We have a variety of plant-based dishes, including vegan sandwiches, oat milk lattes, and avocado toast.

Do you provide free Wi-Fi?

Yes! Just ask our staff for the Wi-Fi password and enjoy unlimited browsing.

Can I make a reservation?

We accept reservations for groups of 4 or more. Call us at 123-456-7890 or book online.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Absolutely! We offer gluten-free bread, pastries, and desserts. Just let us know your preference when ordering.

Do you offer takeaway or delivery?

Yes, you can order takeaway in-store or get delivery through Uber Eats & Deliver
